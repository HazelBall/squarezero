What is Square Zero?

Starting your own business, beginning your freelance career, or just running a side hustle can be daunting. Especially when you have no experience or connections. Whether you've just graduated from high school or college, you've worked a 9-5 job your entire life, or anything in between, it can be hard to know exactly what to do, and what information to trust. That's where Square Zero comes in.

Square Zero is the new up and coming podcast for aspiring entrepreneurs, freelancers, and self-starter. Here, you'll find the tips and tricks, advice, and resources from real people who have failed and succeeded in their own entrepreneurial journeys.
